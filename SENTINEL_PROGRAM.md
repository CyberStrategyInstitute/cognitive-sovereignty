# Sentinel Program

The Sentinel Program recognizes contributors who help harden the Cognitive Sovereignty Framework doctrine. Cognitive security is not a solo discipline — it requires a community of practitioners, researchers, and analysts who hold the framework to a higher standard than any single author can.

---

## Tiers

### ⭐ Supporter
**Requirement:** Star the repository.
**Recognition:** Listed in the community supporters list.

### 🔍 Analyst
**Requirement:** Submit a validated technique entry or CTSS score update that passes red-team review and is merged into the taxonomy.
**Recognition:** Named contributor in MAINTAINERS.md. `Analyst` badge in community spaces.

### 🔬 Researcher
**Requirement:** Submit peer-testable research to `/research/` that changes a CTSS score, adds a technique family, or demonstrates a failure condition in the framework.
**Recognition:** Named contributor in MAINTAINERS.md. Co-attribution in framework documentation where research is cited.

### 🛡️ Sentinel
**Requirement:** Sustained contribution across multiple taxonomy layers — research, technique entries, wargaming results, or edge case identification. Demonstrated commitment to the non-ideological instrumentation standard.
**Recognition:** Priority access to CSI assessment platforms and toolkits. Direct engagement with CSI research team. Named in framework citation where applicable.

---

## How to Qualify

1. Read [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) carefully — especially the red-team standard and what we do not accept.
2. Open an issue before submitting a PR.
3. Contributions are evaluated against the Adversarial Reproduction Test and the Policy Neutrality Test.
4. There is no timeline — quality over velocity.

---

## What Sentinels Are Not

The Sentinel Program is not a loyalty program. It does not reward volume of activity, agreement with the framework's conclusions, or affiliation with any institution. It rewards rigorous, evidence-grounded contributions that make the doctrine more accurate, more complete, and harder to capture by any faction.
