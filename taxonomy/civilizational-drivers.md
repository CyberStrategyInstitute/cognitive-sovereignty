# Layer -2: Civilizational Drivers

> **These are not attacks.** They are structural conditions in society that make attacks effective. They are the root forces that generate vulnerability. Red-team analysis identified this as the most encompassing and slowest-changing layer — but also the most consequential. If these conditions exist, the layers above them will always find expression.

**An adversary does not need to attack when the system self-weaponizes.** CD-001 (Incentive Misalignment) is the Category-Zero condition beneath all others.

---

## Registry

### CD-001 — Incentive Misalignment

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **ID** | CD-001 |
| **Name** | Incentive Misalignment |
| **Detection** | 3 / 5 |
| **Levels** | All |
| **Timeline** | Today |
| **CSF Domains** | Cognitive, Purpose & Moral |

**Description:** Profit, power, relevance, and survival optimized against truth and agency. The attention economy rewards outrage. Bureaucracies reward risk avoidance over truth-telling. Career incentives tie advancement to narrative conformity. Monetization of fear, identity, and grievance creates industries that profit from cognitive degradation.

**Why It Matters:** An adversary does not need to attack when the system self-weaponizes. This is the Category-Zero condition beneath all others. Every other civilizational driver, substrate threat, and active technique finds a more permissive environment when incentive misalignment is present.

**Linked Threat Classes:** TC-01 (Incentive Capture Systems), TC-07 (Moral Reframing Drift)

**CSF Countermeasure:** Domain 5 (Purpose & Moral) — values articulation, resistance to ethical relativism, service orientation that counters convenience-over-virtue drift.

### CD-002 — Information Abundance Collapse

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **ID** | CD-002 |
| **Name** | Information Abundance Collapse |
| **Detection** | 4 / 5 |
| **Levels** | World, Society |
| **Timeline** | 1–2 years |
| **CSF Domains** | Cognitive, Digital & AI Symbiosis |

**Description:** Infinite information with no epistemic hierarchy. When everything is available and nothing is verified, the human mind defaults to shortcuts: trust the tribe, trust the algorithm, trust whatever confirms existing beliefs. Algorithms process statistics and produce probability scores that reshape outcomes, while humans fighting back are held to a far higher standard of evidence.

**Why It Matters:** Creates asymmetric standards of proof favoring automated judgment over human experience. This is procedural reality control — no explicit manipulation required.

**Linked Threat Classes:** TC-02 (Reality Fluidity Mechanisms), TC-05 (Cognitive Overload Saturation)

### CD-003 — Technological Mediation of Reality

| Field | Value |

| **Detection** | 2 / 5 |
| **Levels** | Individual, Family |
| **Timeline** | 3–5 years |

**Description:** Reality is increasingly experienced through systems: algorithms, feeds, AI copilots, augmented reality, and neurotech. This is not a tactic. It is a civilizational phase shift. **Linked Threat Classes:** TC-10 (AI-Mediated Reality Layers), TC-03 (Algorithmic Identity Shaping)

### CD-004 — Institutional Trust Erosion

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **ID** | CD-004 |
| **Name** | Institutional Trust Erosion |
| **Detection** | 4 / 5 |
| **Levels** | USA, US Government |
| **Timeline** | Today |
| **CSF Domains** | Social, Purpose & Moral |

**Description:** Trust in government, media, science, and institutions has declined measurably and persistently. This removes the society-level cognitive immune system. When people cannot rely on institutional verification of claims, they become vulnerable to whoever fills the trust vacuum — including adversaries and algorithms.

**Why It Matters:** A population that does not trust its institutions cannot mount coordinated defense against cognitive manipulation from any source. This is the removal of the collective immune system.

**Measurement:** Trust and Cohesion Index (Layer B societal stability metrics). Reality Legibility Score — can citizens answer: Who made this decision? By what authority? Using what data? With what appeal path?

**Linked Threat Classes:** TC-08 (Institutional Legibility Collapse)

**Red-Team Note:** Added following wargame analysis. Scenarios involving AI swarm evolution outpacing defensive technique updates consistently produced coverage failures that no amount of additional T-CT layer defense could address. The speed mismatch is itself a civilizational vulnerability.

This is the ultimate long-arc threat — and the one with the lowest detection score because effects are slow-moving and culturally normalized before they become crises.

**Linked Outcomes:** HO-003 (Identity Fragmentation), HO-004 (Moral Disengagement)

**Edge Domain Link:** E3 (Civilizational Reproduction Failure)

**Red-Team Note:** Added following wargame scenarios where family dissolution, fertility decline, and intergenerational value transmission failure produced civilizational collapse outcomes that no amount of technical defense could prevent. No adversary action required.

## Measurement: Layer B Societal Stability Metrics

Civilizational Drivers are measured through the Layer B measurement stack:

**B1 — Trust and Cohesion Index:** Institutional trust trends, interpersonal trust surveys, polarization distance, perceived legitimacy of outcomes.

**B2 — Civilizational Reproduction Indicators:** Fertility relative to replacement rate, family stability duration, child wellbeing outcomes, intergenerational value transmission confidence.

**B3 — Reality Legibility Score:** Can citizens answer four questions about decisions affecting them — who decided, by what authority, using what data, with what appeal path?

## Coverage Note

The Civilizational Driver layer is the most encompassing and slowest-changing. Coverage at this layer is approximately **90%**, with the primary remaining gap being detection of long-arc civilizational degradation before it crosses irreversible thresholds (CD-006, CD-005).

These conditions cannot be addressed by military doctrine alone. They require a whole-of-society approach. The CSF positions DoD personnel and their families to withstand civilizational-level degradation while broader corrections are pursued.
